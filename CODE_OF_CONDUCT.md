# Code of Conduct

## Standard

HBN is an open protocol project. Participants are expected to act with technical honesty, respect, and restraint.

## Expected behavior

- discuss ideas directly and with evidence
- critique claims, code, and design choices without attacking people
- keep safety, clarity, and attribution visible
- acknowledge uncertainty where it exists
- respect contributors across languages, regions, and experience levels

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- abusive or discriminatory language
- deliberate misrepresentation of technical facts
- repeated bad-faith disruption of reviews or discussions
- publication of private information without consent

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, and discussions to protect the project and its participants. Serious or repeated violations may result in removal from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the contact path described in `SUPPORT.md`.
